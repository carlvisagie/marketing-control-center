# JUST TALK - ORGANIC SALES STRATEGY PLAYBOOK

## THE CORE PROBLEM (What Went Wrong)

**40,000 paid ad views → 0 sales**

This happened because:
1. **Paid reach ≠ Interested buyers** - Meta optimizes for "views" not "purchases"
2. **Cold traffic to unknown brand** - No trust built
3. **Price mismatch** - Ads showed $29, landing page showed $5.99
4. **Wrong audience** - Passive scrollers, not people in pain

**The fix:** Organic content that speaks to PAIN, builds TRUST, and drives ACTION.

## TARGET AUDIENCE BREAKDOWN

- Can't burden family with what they see
- Irregular schedules (3 AM is their 3 PM)
- High burnout, high emotional load

**Pain points to address:**

## THE ORGANIC STRATEGY

### PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Week 1-2)

**Goal:** Establish presence, build content library

**Actions:**
1. Post 1 feed post daily (use templates provided)
2. Post 2-3 stories daily with link stickers
3. Engage with 20 posts in target hashtags daily
4. Follow 50 accounts in target audiences daily
5. Respond to every comment and DM

**Content mix:**
- 40% Pain point posts (3 AM, anxiety, loneliness)
- 30% Target audience posts (nurses, vets, moms)
- 20% Social proof (testimonials, stats)
- 10% Offer posts (pricing, features)

---

### PHASE 2: ENGAGEMENT (Week 3-4)

**Goal:** Build community, drive conversations

**Actions:**
1. Use question stickers in stories daily
2. Create polls about mental health topics
3. Respond to story replies with personalized messages
4. Share user-generated content (with permission)
5. Go live once a week (even 5-10 minutes)

**Engagement tactics:**
- Reply to comments with questions to continue conversation
- DM people who engage heavily (not sales pitch, just connection)
- Comment meaningfully on posts in target hashtags

---

### PHASE 3: CONVERSION (Week 5+)

**Goal:** Turn engagement into sales

**Actions:**
1. Add countdown stickers for offer deadlines
2. Share more testimonials and results
3. Create urgency with limited-time pricing
4. Direct message warm leads with soft CTA
5. Retarget engaged users with stories

**Conversion tactics:**
- "I noticed you've been engaging with our content. Just wanted to say hi and see if you have any questions about Just Talk."
- "Hey! Saw you voted on our poll. If you ever need someone to talk to, we're here 24/7. No pressure."

---

## CONTENT PILLARS

### Pillar 1: PAIN (40% of content)
Speak directly to the emotional pain your audience feels.

Examples:
- "It's 3 AM. Your thoughts won't stop."
- "The loneliest feeling isn't being alone."
- "I'm fine." The most common lie we tell.

### Pillar 2: PERMISSION (25% of content)
Give people permission to need help.

Examples:
- "You don't have to earn the right to be supported."
- "You don't need a 'good enough' reason."
- "You deserve to be heard too."

### Pillar 3: PROOF (20% of content)
Show that it works.

Examples:
- Testimonials (real or composite)
- Statistics about mental health
- Comparisons (therapy cost vs Just Talk)

### Pillar 4: PRODUCT (15% of content)
Explain what you offer.

Examples:
- How it works
- Pricing
- Features
- FAQ

## THE FIRST SALE STRATEGY

To get your FIRST sale, focus on:

1. **Personal outreach** - DM 10 people per day who engage with mental health content. Not a sales pitch, just genuine connection.

2. **Story link stickers** - Post 3+ stories daily with direct links. This bypasses the "link in bio" friction.

3. **Urgency** - "Launch special ends in 7 days" creates FOMO.

4. **Testimonials** - Even if you have to create composite testimonials based on what you KNOW users would say.

5. **Target the desperate** - People posting about anxiety, insomnia, loneliness at 2 AM. They need this NOW.
