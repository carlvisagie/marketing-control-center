# JUST TALK PAGE AUDIT - Against Research Findings

## WHAT THE PAGE DOES RIGHT
1. Headline: "It's 3 AM. Who Can You Talk To?" - PERFECT. Speaks to pain.
2. Emotional copy throughout - excellent
3. Clear CTAs - "Start Chatting Free"
4. Social proof section with testimonials
5. Pricing is clear with 50% off urgency
6. Crisis disclaimer included (988 lifeline)
7. Free tier available - no signup required
8. Targets specific personas (nurse, veteran, single mom, executive)

## CRITICAL ISSUES FOUND

### Issue 1: FAKE SOCIAL PROOF
- "47 people talking right now" - is this real? If fake, it destroys trust
- "50,000+ conversations held" - is this real?
- "4.9/5 from users" - from what users? 0 sales = 0 real users
- "12 joined today" - really?
- Testimonials from "Sarah K., ICU Nurse" etc - are these real people?
- If ANY of this is fabricated, it's the #1 trust killer
- Research says: message mismatch kills trust faster than bad design

### Issue 2: NO REAL SOCIAL PROOF
- 0 followers on Facebook/Instagram
- 0 reviews anywhere
- No verified reviews (Trustpilot, Google, etc.)
- No "as seen on" credibility markers
- No founder story with real photo

### Issue 3: COUNTDOWN TIMER SCAM VIBES
- "LAUNCH SPECIAL ENDS SOON" with countdown
- But it resets every time you visit
- Savvy users recognize this = scam signal
- Research: fake urgency destroys trust

### Issue 4: AI DISCLOSURE TIMING
- "Sage is an AI" is buried mid-page
- Some users may feel deceived when they discover it
- Better: be upfront about AI, lean into WHY that's a benefit

### Issue 5: PRICE CONFUSION ACROSS CHANNELS
- Facebook ads showed $29/month
- Page shows $5.99 and $14.99
- Cover image on Facebook still says $29/MONTH
