# JUST TALK - HIGH-CONVERTING CONTENT TEMPLATES

## The Psychology Behind These Templates

These templates use proven persuasion principles:
- **Pain-Agitation-Solution (PAS)** - Identify the pain, agitate it, offer the solution
- **Emotional resonance** - Speak to feelings, not features
- **Specificity** - "3 AM" is more powerful than "late at night"
- **Direct response** - Every post ends with a clear call to action

## KEY RULES FOR EVERY POST

1. **End with CTA** - "Link in bio" on EVERY post
2. **Speak to pain** - Don't list features, describe feelings
3. **Be specific** - "3 AM" not "late at night"
4. **No hard sell** - Offer relief, not a product
5. **Use purple heart 💜** - Brand consistency
6. **Post late** - 8 PM - 11 PM when people are vulnerable
