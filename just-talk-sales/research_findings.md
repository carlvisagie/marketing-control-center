# RESEARCH FINDINGS: Why Just Talk Has Zero Sales

## KEY RESEARCH DATA

### 1. AI Mental Health Chatbot Market Reality (Jan 2026 Survey)
- **35.25%** of Americans choose AI chatbots over therapists due to FEAR OF JUDGMENT (not cost)
- **43.75%** prefer AI as their FIRST response to mental health issues
- **38%** use AI chatbots WEEKLY for emotional support
- **64.25%** report moderate to major improvement
- **30.5%** say financial stress is biggest mental health trigger
- **1 in 3** people use AI chatbots for mental health

### 2. Trust is THE Barrier (Stanford HAI, YouGov)
- Stanford study: AI therapy chatbots show stigma and can give dangerous responses
- Trust is the #1 missing ingredient for AI mental health tools
- People need to feel SAFE before paying
- Bigger models don't fix trust - only human connection does

### 3. Landing Page Conversion Research (Reddit SaaS)
- Message mismatch between ad and page kills trust instantly
- One person changed ONLY headline to match ad = 1.8% to 4.2% conversion
- Pages that try to do too much fail
- Headlines must address PAIN not features
- Mobile experience is critical
- "Pretty doesn't mean effective" - clarity beats creativity

### 4. First 10 Customers (Stripe Atlas Guide)
- "If you build it, they will do absolutely nothing"
- Must ACTIVELY recruit first customers one at a time
- Start with personal network
- Warm introductions > cold pitches
- Find where customers congregate online
- Early adopters are key
- Don't buy leads - develop them yourself

### 5. Why Good Products Get Zero Sales
- Wrong traffic source (paid views ≠ buyers)
- No social proof (0 reviews, 0 followers)
- Trust deficit (new brand, AI product, sensitive topic)
- No free trial that builds habit before paywall
- Price shown in ads didn't match landing page

## DIAGNOSIS: WHY JUST TALK HAS ZERO SALES

### Problem 1: TRUST DEFICIT
- Brand new page with 0 followers, 0 reviews
- AI therapy is a HIGH-TRUST purchase
- People sharing deepest feelings need to trust first
- No testimonials, no social proof, no credibility markers

### Problem 2: WRONG TRAFFIC
- Paid Facebook/Instagram ads to cold audiences
- People scrolling social media aren't in "I need therapy" mode
- Ad optimized for views/reach, not conversions
- Price mismatch between ads ($29) and page ($5.99)

### Problem 3: NO RELATIONSHIP BEFORE ASKING FOR MONEY
- Going straight from "never heard of you" to "give me money"
- Research shows: people need 7-12 touches before buying
- Especially for mental health - trust must be earned

### Problem 4: FREE TIER DOESN'T CREATE HABIT
- Free chat exists but no clear path to "I need more"
- No urgency, no scarcity, no reason to upgrade NOW

## WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS (Evidence-Based)

### Strategy 1: GO WHERE THE PAIN IS (Free)
- Reddit: r/anxiety, r/depression, r/lonely, r/insomnia
- Facebook Groups: anxiety support, caregiver support, nurse burnout
- Don't sell - HELP. Answer questions. Be present.
- When people ask "what helps?" mention Just Talk naturally

### Strategy 2: GIVE IT AWAY TO BUILD PROOF (Free)
- Offer 10 people FREE premium access for 30 days
- In exchange: honest review/testimonial
- Those testimonials become social proof
- Target: nurses, caregivers, veterans, new moms

### Strategy 3: CONTENT THAT SPEAKS TO PAIN (Free)
- Post at 10 PM - 2 AM when target audience is awake and struggling
- Content about the FEELING not the product
- "It's 3 AM. Your thoughts won't stop. You don't want to burden anyone."
- Let the pain do the selling

### Strategy 4: MICRO-INFLUENCERS (Free/Cheap)
- Find mental health advocates with 1k-10k followers
- Offer free access in exchange for honest review
- Their audience TRUSTS them already

### Strategy 5: PARTNER WITH THERAPISTS (Free)
- Therapists have waitlists
- Position Just Talk as "between sessions support"
- Not replacement - supplement
- Therapists refer overflow clients

### Strategy 6: FREE VALUE FIRST (Free)
- Create free mental health resources
- Guided breathing exercises
- Sleep hygiene checklist
- Anxiety grounding techniques
- Capture emails → nurture → convert


## HOW SUCCESSFUL AI MENTAL HEALTH APPS GOT USERS

### Woebot (Millions of users)
- Published PEER-REVIEWED RESEARCH proving it works
- Clinical credibility = institutional trust = partnerships
- Evidence-based design (CBT)

### Wysa (Millions of downloads, World Economic Forum recognition)
- HYBRID MODEL: AI + human therapists
- Scaled through B2B: employers, insurers, healthcare providers
- NOT dependent on individual downloads
- Localized content for multiple regions

### Replika (10M+ users)
- Positioned as AI FRIEND/COMPANION (not therapy)
- Emotional design + personalization
- Social virality + app store visibility
- Gamified engagement

### SHARED GROWTH STRATEGIES:
1. Clinical credibility (research/evidence)
2. Employer/insurer partnerships (B2B)
3. Emotional engagement (not clinical branding)
4. LOW-FRICTION ONBOARDING (no payment upfront)
5. Global accessibility
6. Community trust + organic word-of-mouth

### KEY INSIGHT FOR JUST TALK:
- NONE of these apps grew through Facebook/Instagram ads
- They grew through: clinical validation, B2B partnerships, app store optimization, word-of-mouth
- The successful ones DON'T look like "therapy" - they look like a FRIEND
