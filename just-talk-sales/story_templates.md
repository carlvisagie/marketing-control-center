- **Direct link stickers** - No "link in bio" friction
- **Full-screen attention** - No scrolling distractions

## STORY DESIGN TIPS

### Colors to Use
- **Primary:** Deep purple (#6B46C1)
- **Secondary:** Soft pink/purple gradient
- **Text:** White or light cream
- **Accents:** Purple heart emoji 💜

### Fonts
- Clean, modern sans-serif
- Large, readable text
- Center-aligned for impact

### Timing
- **Text slides:** 3-5 seconds
- **Interactive slides:** 10+ seconds (let people engage)
- **CTA slides:** 5 seconds minimum

### Best Times to Post Stories
- **10 PM - 12 AM** (when people can't sleep)
- **6 AM - 8 AM** (morning anxiety)
- **12 PM - 1 PM** (lunch break stress)

## LINK STICKER BEST PRACTICES

1. **Make it visible** - Place in lower third of screen
2. **Use action words** - "Talk Now" not "Learn More"
3. **Add arrow pointing to it** - Draw attention
4. **Customize text** - Don't use default "Link"
5. **Test the link** - Make sure it works before posting

### Recommended Link Sticker Text:
- "Start Talking Free"
- "Talk to Someone"
- "Get 50% Off"
- "Try It Now"
- "Be Heard"
- "Talk Now"
